Anonymous by default

Find someone for the next thing on campus.

Plus One helps students turn small campus moments into low-pressure meetups: lunch before class, a study sprint, a sports event, or a quick walk across campus.

Temporary identity

No account, password, or public profile is required. Each browser session gets a temporary identity that can create posts, swipe on plans, match, and chat.

Fast matching

Activity cards expire quickly, so Discover stays focused on plans that are happening soon. A right swipe opens a five-minute anonymous chat.

Private by design

Public cards show only the plan, time, and campus location. Chats stay between matched users, and safety checks help keep posts and messages appropriate.

How it works

From plan to handoff in one short flow.

  1. Create a time-limited Plus One card for something you already want to do.
  2. Other anonymous sessions discover the card and swipe if they are interested.
  3. A match opens a short chat so both people can check the vibe quickly.
  4. If both agree, Plus One shows the meeting place, time, and a safety reminder.

What stays hidden

Anonymous does not mean unstructured.

Your temporary identity is tied to this browser session only. Other people see your activity card and your chat messages after matching, but not your account record, session key, or private browser identity.

no sign-up browser session short-lived cards matched chat only

Built for campus timing

Plus One is for plans that are easier with one more person.

Instead of building a permanent social profile, the app keeps the focus on the immediate plan: what it is, where it happens, when it starts, and whether both people feel comfortable meeting.
